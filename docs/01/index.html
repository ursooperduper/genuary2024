Day 1: Particles. Lots of particles.

Return home

The piece is called Gobstopper as it reminds me of layers of color that appear as you suck on a gobstopper candy.

I'm using this Genuary as a means to get acquainted with P5js and explore some of its libraries. For this first prompt, I'm using the P5.particles library to create a series of multi-colored elliptical particles in bright colors. Each particle trail overlaps the next making it appears as though it's snaking across the screen. Next, I used P5.layers library to duplicate the series of particles across multiple rectangular areas of the screen.
